Our Story

Founded in 2020, The Coffee Corner was born from a passion for community and a perfect brew. We are a family-run café dedicated to bringing you the finest ethically sourced beans from local roasters, paired with delicious homemade pastries baked fresh daily.

Our space is designed to be your home away from home – whether you're catching up with friends, getting some work done, or simply enjoying a moment of peace with a great book and a flat white.

We believe in sustainability, quality, and most importantly, our wonderful customers.
